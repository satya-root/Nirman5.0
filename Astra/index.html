About SevaBandhu

SevaBandhu is an intelligent hospital staff-management platform designed for India’s high-volume healthcare system. It solves the major challenges of manual attendance, shift scheduling, emergency alerts, communication breakdowns, and staff tracking. With Aadhaar/QR attendance, real-time duty monitoring, instant Code Blue/Red alerts, leave automation, and multilingual communication, SevaBandhu ensures the right staff reach the right place at the right time.

Key Features

Aadhaar/QR Attendance

Instant, secure digital attendance for all staff members.

Auto Shift Scheduling

Smart algorithm assigns duties, prevents overwork & understaffing.

Emergency Alerts

Instant Code Blue/Red notifications to all on-duty staff.

AI Predictions

Forecast staffing needs based on patient inflow trends.

IoT Integration

Track ICU/OT environment and real-time staff locations.

Affordable for Indian Hospitals

₹20–₹50 per staff monthly with low onboarding cost.
