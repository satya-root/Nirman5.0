How CrowdResolve works

Behind a simple interface, CrowdResolve uses AI to reduce manual triage work for officials and increase visibility for citizens.

  1. Citizens submit an issue with description, location, and optional photo.
  2. AI categorises the issue, suggests priority, and routes it to the correct department.
  3. Officials view structured queues, apply filters, and update status.
  4. Actions are tracked as Open, In Progress, Resolved, or Rejected.
  5. Citizens track real-time progress in their personal dashboards.
